Vision-Based Solver

Shoebox Tasks have a clear visual structure, making them well suited for automated vision-based analysis. The vision-based solver analyzes images of a task, creates its PDDL representation, and generates an action plan that enables the robot to solve the task.

The system supports two main use cases. In the first, the robot acts as a teacher, guiding the student through the task and monitoring progress. In the second, the student controls the robot through the visual programming interface, learning programming concepts in the familiar context of Shoebox Tasks and visual schedules.
