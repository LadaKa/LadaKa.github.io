Robot-Assisted Shoebox Tasks

Teaching with robots and teaching robots through tasks designed for the education of individuals with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD).
This PhD research project explores automated task solving using vision-based machine learning, enabling robots to perceive, interpret, and execute structured tasks autonomously, as well as through a visual programming interface.
